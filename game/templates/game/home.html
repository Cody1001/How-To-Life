How to Life!?

Welcome!

How to Life!? is a short multiple choice game.

You will be presented with a series of situations, each of which will contain a question of a moral nature. Answers will be multiple choice and each answer will be worth a set amount of karma(points).

Really good decisions will result in getting lots of karma. Really bad decisions will result in losing karma. Neutral decisions will net zero karma.

You can try to get as much karma as possible or lose as much karma as possible. We're not here to judge. Acutally we are, at end of the game we are definitely going to judge you. Whether we have nice things to say or not, is up to you.
